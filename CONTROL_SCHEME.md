# Mage Control Scheme

## The Emissary's Way: Left Side = Left Click

The Mage is the **Emissary**—reactive, sequential, action-oriented. The left brain.

### Controls

| Action | Input | Behavior |
|--------|-------|----------|
| **Quick Cast** | Left-click tap | Immediately casts last selected spell |
| **View Spells** | Left-click hold | Orbital menu appears showing all spells |
| **Choose Spell** | Move mouse while holding | Highlight moves to spell under cursor |
| **Cast Spell** | Release left-click | Selected spell is cast at cursor position |
| **Spell Editor** | `M` key | Opens full spell configuration editor |

### Philosophy

> *"The emissary is reactive—tap to fire, or hold to see options. Quick action, immediate execution. The spell is cast outward into the world."*

The left brain processes:
- Sequential actions
- Logical execution
- Outward action

This maps to the Mage's role: casting **spells** (discrete actions) that project your will onto the page, building the constellation that leads to convergence.

### Spell Mechanics

- **Yang spells**: Assertive, protective, closing the privacy gap
- **Yin spells**: Receptive, revealing, understanding the landscape

Each spell cast:
1. Adds a node to your constellation
2. Mutates the hexagram state
3. Moves you closer to convergence with the Swordsman

### The Orbital Interface

When you hold left-click, spells appear in orbit around your cursor:
- The **teal glow** indicates the Mage's color
- Move outward from center to select
- The center shows the currently highlighted spell
- Release to cast
